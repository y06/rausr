### Adobe XD Was (and Still Is) Good

Adobe XD launched with a clean, fast interface. Lightweight. Simple. **Loved by many.**

XD also struggled with collaboration features; its cloud sync and coediting lagged behind Figma‚Äôs real-time editing, causing teams to stick with Figma or other tools.
